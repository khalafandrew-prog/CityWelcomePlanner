Welcome to London

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom. The city is filled with historic landmarks, world-class museums, beautiful parks and amazing food from all over the world.

Key City Info

  • Currency: British Pound Sterling (£)
  • Country code: +44 (0)
  • Time zone: GMT / BST in summer
  • Plugs: Type G, 3-pin
  • Transport: TfL – Underground, Overground, DLR, buses, trains
  • Payment: Contactless card, phone pay, or Oyster

Attractions & Landmarks

Big Ben & Westminster

Iconic clock tower, Parliament and Westminster Abbey by the River Thames.

Nearest Tube: Westminster (Circle, District, Jubilee)

Good for: Photos, history, river walks

London Eye

Observation wheel with panoramic views across central London.

Nearest Tube: Waterloo / Westminster

Tip: Book online to avoid queues.

Tower of London & Tower Bridge

Medieval fortress, Crown Jewels and one of London’s most famous bridges.

Nearest Tube: Tower Hill

Good for: Families, history fans.

Museums

British Museum

World art and artefacts – many galleries free.

Nearest Tube: Holborn / Tottenham Court Road

Natural History Museum

Dinosaurs, blue whale, volcanoes & more.

Nearest Tube: South Kensington

Science Museum & V&A

Interactive science exhibits and design / fashion collections.

Nearest Tube: South Kensington

Family Activities

Family Favourites

  • Sea Life London Aquarium (South Bank)
  • London Zoo (Regent’s Park)
  • Shrek’s Adventure / London Dungeon (South Bank)
  • Harry Potter Studio Tour (Watford – book ahead)

Tips with Kids

  • Use contactless/Oyster – kids under certain ages travel free on buses & Tube (check rules).
  • Plan toilet & snack stops around big stations and museums.
  • Keep an eye on closing times – many attractions close around 17:00–18:00.

Parks & Gardens

Hyde Park & Kensington Gardens

Huge central park for walks, cycling and boating on the Serpentine.

Nearest Tube: Hyde Park Corner / Lancaster Gate

Regent’s Park & Primrose Hill

Gardens, London Zoo and a hilltop view of the skyline.

Nearest Tube: Baker Street / Regent’s Park

Greenwich Park

Royal Observatory, meridian line and views over Canary Wharf.

Nearest: Greenwich rail / DLR

Shopping Areas

Oxford Street & Regent Street

Flagship high-street shops and famous department stores.

Nearest Tube: Oxford Circus / Bond Street / Tottenham Court Road

Covent Garden

Boutiques, market stalls, street performers and restaurants.

Nearest Tube: Covent Garden / Leicester Square

Westfield (Stratford & Shepherd’s Bush)

Large indoor malls with fashion, food courts and cinemas.

Nearest: Stratford / Shepherd’s Bush

Emergency & Embassy Help

UK Emergency Numbers

  • 999 – Police, Ambulance, Fire (emergency only)
  • 112 – European emergency number (works in UK)
  • 111 – Medical help, not life-threatening
  • 101 – Non-emergency police

If in doubt about danger to life, property or someone’s safety, call 999.
