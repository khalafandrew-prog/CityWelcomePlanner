About London

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom. The city is filled with historic landmarks, world-class museums, beautiful parks and amazing food from all over the world.

Key City Info

  • Currency: British Pound Sterling (£)
  • Country code: +44 (0)
  • Time zone: GMT / BST in summer
  • Plugs: Type G, 3-pin
  • Transport: TfL – Underground, Overground, DLR, buses, trains
  • Payment: Contactless card, phone pay, or Oyster

Airports & Transport to Central London

London is served by several airports. These cards show typical train options into central London. Always check live prices & times before you travel.

Airport

Heathrow (LHR)

West London • Underground + fast trains
Zone 1-6

Heathrow Express

From: London Paddington

To: Heathrow Terminals 2/3, 4, 5

Duration: 15–20 minutes

Approx fare: from £22 (single)

Elizabeth line (TfL Rail)

From: Paddington, Bond Street, Tottenham Court Road, Liverpool Street

Duration: 30–50 minutes

Payment: Contactless / Oyster

Piccadilly line

From: Piccadilly line stations (e.g. King’s Cross, Piccadilly Circus)

Duration: ~50 minutes from Zone 1

Best for: Cheapest direct Tube route

Always use the correct ticket for each service – tickets are not interchangeable between operators.
Airport

Gatwick (LGW)

South of London • Fast trains to Victoria / London Bridge
Outside Zones

Gatwick Express

From: London Victoria

To: Gatwick Airport

Duration: 30–35 minutes

Southern

From: London Victoria

To: Gatwick Airport

Duration: ~35 minutes

Often cheaper than Express.

Thameslink

From: St Pancras Intl / London Bridge / Blackfriars

To: Gatwick Airport

Best for: Direct from King’s Cross / St Pancras area

Gatwick services may not accept Oyster – use contactless or a rail ticket.
Airport

Luton (LTN)

North of London • Shuttle bus from Parkway station
Outside Zones

East Midlands / Thameslink

From: St Pancras Intl / Farringdon / Blackfriars

To: Luton Airport Parkway

Duration: 25–35 minutes

Shuttle bus

From: Luton Airport Parkway station

To: Luton Airport terminal

Duration: ~10 minutes

Notes

Train tickets usually include the shuttle bus. Check your ticket details before boarding.

Trains use National Rail fares – Oyster is not valid beyond the London boundary.
Airport

Stansted (STN)

North-East of London • Fast train from Liverpool Street
Outside Zones

Stansted Express

From: London Liverpool Street

To: Stansted Airport

Duration: ~45 minutes

Notes

Advance tickets can be significantly cheaper than buying on the day.

Tip

For early flights, check first/last train times carefully.

Airport

London City (LCY)

East London • DLR direct to Bank / Stratford
Zone 3

DLR (Docklands Light Railway)

From: Bank / Tower Gateway / Stratford

To: London City Airport

Duration: ~25–35 minutes

Payment: Contactless / Oyster

Best for

Business travellers and short-haul flights with hand luggage.

DLR is part of TfL – standard Tube/rail fares apply within Zones 1-3.

Museums

Many London museums are free for their main collections. Some special exhibitions charge for entry.

British Museum – Bloomsbury

World-famous collection of art & artefacts. Free entry to main galleries.

Nearest Tube: Holborn / Tottenham Court Road

Natural History Museum – South Kensington

Dinosaurs, nature and science. Ideal for children.

Nearest Tube: South Kensington

Science Museum – South Kensington

Interactive exhibits and IMAX. Free entry.

V&A Museum – South Kensington

Art, design & fashion from around the world.

Parks & Gardens

Emergency & Embassy Help

UK Emergency Numbers

  • 999: Police, Ambulance, Fire (life-threatening emergency)
  • 112: European emergency number (works like 999)
  • 111: Non-emergency medical advice (NHS)

When calling, clearly state your location (street name, nearby landmark or postcode).
