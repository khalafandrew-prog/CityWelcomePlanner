About London

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom. The city is filled with historic landmarks, world-class museums, beautiful parks and amazing food from all over the world.

Key City Info

  • Currency: British Pound Sterling (£)
  • Country code: +44 (0)
  • Time zone: GMT / BST in summer
  • Plugs: Type G, 3-pin
  • Transport: TfL – Underground, Overground, DLR, buses, trains
  • Payment: Contactless card, phone pay, or Oyster

Emergency & Embassies

UK Emergency Numbers

  • 999 – Emergency (police, ambulance, fire)
  • 112 – EU-wide emergency number (also works in UK)
  • 101 – Non-emergency police
  • 111 – NHS non-emergency medical help

When calling from a foreign mobile, you can still dial 999 or 112 without a UK SIM.

Safety Tips

  • Beware of pickpockets in crowded tourist areas and on public transport.
  • Use licensed black cabs or registered minicabs; avoid unmarked cars.
  • Keep bags closed and in sight when sitting in cafés and restaurants.
  • Drinking alcohol is not allowed on most TfL public transport.
